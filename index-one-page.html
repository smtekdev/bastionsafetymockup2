About Us

About Bastion Safety Consulting

Bastion Safety Consulting was founded with one mission — helping businesses create safer, stronger, and more knowledgeable workplaces.

We believe safety training should go beyond presentations and paperwork. Our approach combines real-world field experience with modern training methods to ensure employees fully understand workplace hazards, compliance requirements, and safe work practices.

From safety audits and inspections to customized training programs and risk assessments, we help companies build confidence, reduce incidents, and maintain compliance with current federal and state safety standards.
